## Incident Response

### Severity Levels

| Level | Definition | Response Time | Examples |
|-------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| P1 - Critical | Service down, data loss risk | 15 min | DB unreachable, corruption |
| P2 - High | Degraded performance, partial failure | 1 hour | Slow queries, high error rate |
| P3 - Medium | Non-critical issue, workaround exists | 4 hours | Single source failing |
| P4 - Low | Cosmetic, informational | Next business day | Log noise, minor config |

### Response Procedures

1. **Acknowledge** — Confirm alert receipt
2. **Assess** — Determine scope and severity
3. **Communicate** — Notify stakeholders if P1/P2
4. **Mitigate** — Apply immediate fix or workaround
5. **Resolve** — Implement permanent fix
6. **Review** — Post-incident review and documentation
